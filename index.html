About Me

Mathematician, researcher, and educator at VIT Chennai.

I am a mathematician and data scientist who works at the intersection of rigorous theory, scientific computing, and modern machine learning—turning ideas in linear algebra, graph theory, and fuzzy logic into practical tools for research, teaching, and institutional quality assurance.

I serve as Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics, School of Advanced Sciences, VIT Chennai, and as Assistant Director for Ranking and Accreditation. My research spans generalized inverses and matrix theory, graph embeddings and network science, fuzzy and intuitionistic fuzzy decision models, and deep learning for image processing and healthcare analytics. I publish in applied mathematics and interdisciplinary venues, collaborate with doctoral scholars, and contribute to national and international ranking and accreditation frameworks.

Beyond research, I design software and workflows that reduce manual effort in academic administration—from NBA data validation and course-outcome tracking to timetable scheduling and result analytics. I regularly deliver faculty development programmes and industry-oriented training in Python, R, LaTeX/Typst, data science, machine learning, and generative AI, and I teach undergraduate and postgraduate courses with an emphasis on clarity, computation, and outcome-based education.
